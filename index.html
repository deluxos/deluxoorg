deluxo (DLUX)
an F&F token

Deluxo is a cryptocurrency that operates on the Ethereum blockchain by using the ERC-20 token standard. What makes Deluxo special is the amount of time it has been on the Ethereum blockchain. Deluxo was deployed roughly years ago and predates all of these new tokens that have exploded in popularity. By having such a longtime residency, Deluxo is able to establish itself over any emerging, dubious tokens.

The purpose of Deluxo has changed over the years, from giving gratuity to friends and family, to introducing newcomers to the world of cryptocurrency, to acting as a fungible collectible with a cool name. No matter what the purpose, Deluxo will always be for everyone in cryptocurrency scene.

Why does it take Ethereum to transfer Deluxo?
Deluxo is an ERC-20 token. This means that is does not have it's own blockchain (a system of verifying transactions, mining, etc.) so it must operate under a larger one (the Ethereum blockchain). Please remember: Ethereum is needed to pay for Deluxo transactions and only Ethereum addresses can recieve Deluxo.

How much Deluxo do you plan distribute?
We plan to distribute 4,621,100 DLUX out of the total 4,700,000 DLUX. This will be accomplished primarily through a multi-token sender distributor.

How much is 1 DLUX worth?
Deluxo currently has no real value. It is worth only what you value it. If we evenly distribute the 4,621,100 DLUX as planned, we will eventually be on cryptocurrency exchanges.
